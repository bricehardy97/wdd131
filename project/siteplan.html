Site Plan for Moose's Ice Hut

Welcome to the site plan document for Moose's Ice Hut – the best place for refreshing snowcones!

Site Name

Moose's Ice Hut

This name represents a local, friendly shack where customers can enjoy delicious and refreshing snowcones. It's catchy, easy to remember, and aligns with the fun and casual nature of the business.

Optional domain availability: moosesicehut.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to promote Moose's Ice Hut and provide information about the menu, location, and seasonal specials. The website will serve as a hub for snowcone lovers, allowing customers to check the menu, place orders, and learn about upcoming events.

Scenarios

  • What is the menu for Moose's Ice Hut, and what are the most popular snowcone flavors?
  • What are the operating hours and location of Moose's Ice Hut?
  • Does Moose's Ice Hut offer catering services for events?

Color Schema

The selected color scheme for Moose's Ice Hut is vibrant and refreshing, just like our snowcones!

  • #5ac8ff (Blue): Used for Headers
  • #07d140 (Green): Used for footers

These colors will be used throughout the website to create a fun, vibrant look.

Typography

The typography for the site should be easy to read and match the fun, casual vibe of the snowcone shack.

  • Font 1: Roboto – Used for body text and general content.
  • Font 2: Pacifico – Used for headings to give a playful and friendly feel.
